Approach

Fated AI is a research-driven proprietary trading firm. We focus on short-duration opportunities in liquid digital-asset markets, where structure, liquidity, and attention all move on compressed timeframes.

The work sits at the intersection of quantitative research, real-time data infrastructure, and disciplined risk management. We run both discretionary and systematic strategies, and we hold both to the same calibration standard.

Research

Hypotheses are tested against live market data before they become positions. We treat data quality as a first-order problem.

Execution

Discovery and entry are automated; review is human. Latency and slippage are budgeted, not assumed.

Risk

Dollar-denominated limits, hard kill switches, weekly post-mortems. The losing source always exists — we find it and weight it down.
