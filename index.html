Fated AI is a research-driven proprietary trading firm. We focus on short-duration opportunities in liquid digital-asset markets, where structure, liquidity, and attention all move on compressed timeframes.

Approach

The work sits at the intersection of quantitative research, real-time data infrastructure, and disciplined risk management.

We run both discretionary and systematic strategies, and we hold both to the same calibration standard. Hypotheses are tested against live market data before they become positions. We treat data quality as a first-order problem.

  1. 01

    Research

    Hypotheses tested against live market data before they become positions. Source quality and attribution are first-order problems, not afterthoughts.

  2. 02

    Execution

    Discovery and entry are automated; review is human. Latency and slippage are budgeted, not assumed. Discretion is allowed, then audited.

  3. 03

    Risk

    Dollar-denominated limits, hard kill switches, weekly post-mortems. The losing source always exists — we find it and weight it down.
