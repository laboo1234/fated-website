Fated AI is a proprietary trading firm. We find edge in short-duration opportunities in liquid digital-asset markets, and execute it with software we build ourselves. AI is the engineering layer that lets a single operator run at the cadence and depth of a small team.

Approach

The firm rests on three pillars — edge, execution, and the engineering layer that makes both possible at unusual operator scale.

We don't claim to predict markets. We find candidates earlier than the median operator, weigh what we see honestly, and execute with a structured layer that treats latency, slippage, and risk as first-order problems. The trading engine is built and operated in-house; AI does the engineering volume that a solo operator otherwise couldn't sustain.

  1. 01

    Edge

    Multi-source signal aggregation. Per-source attribution. Two-proof discipline before conviction. A closed Improvement loop that tunes parameters from measured outcomes, not intuition.

  2. 02

    Execution

    Sub-second discovery to fill. Kill switches in code, not policy. Reserve discipline that protects capital after profit. Discretion is permitted, then audited.

  3. 03

    AI

    An agent fleet builds and maintains the trading engine. An AI acting-COO runs operations, strategy, and governance. The operator merges; nothing auto-ships. AI is the engineering layer, not the alpha.
